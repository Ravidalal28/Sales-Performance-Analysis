# 📊 Superstore Sales Performance Analysis

## 🧾 Project Overview
This project analyzes retail sales performance using the **Superstore dataset from Kaggle**.  
The goal is to uncover insights related to **sales trends, profitability patterns, regional performance, product performance, and seasonality** to support data-driven business decisions.

Dataset:  
https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/bhanupratapbiswas/superstore-sales

---

## 🎯 Objectives
- Clean and prepare sales and order data for analysis
- Calculate key business KPIs
- Analyze sales performance across:
  - Regions
  - Categories & Sub-categories
  - Products & Cities
  - Time (monthly & seasonal trends)
- Identify top and bottom performing products
- Study shipping behavior and delivery delays
- Generate actionable business insights

## 📁 Project Workflow

### 1. Data Cleaning
- Removed duplicate records

## 📈 Key Insights
- Sales show strong **seasonality patterns** across months.
- A small number of products contribute to a large portion of revenue (Pareto principle).
- Sales performance varies significantly across regions.
- Shipping mode impacts delivery speed and efficiency.
- Customer value and order behavior vary widely, indicating segmentation opportunities.

---

## 🚀 Business Recommendations
1. Focus marketing efforts on high-performing regions and replicate strategies in low-performing ones.
2. Optimize inventory for top-selling products to avoid stockouts.
3. Improve logistics efficiency for shipping modes with high delays.
4. Promote underperforming categories with targeted discounts and campaigns.
5. Introduce customer segmentation strategies to increase retention and lifetime value.
